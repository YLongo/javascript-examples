Running Tips

Stength

Strength training is important for runners. It helps to improve your overall fitness and can prevent injuries.

Cadence

Running at the right cadence can help to improve your overall fitness and can prevent injuries.

Change is up

Don't run in the same place every time. Change your route every week. Change your pace.

Focus on form

Run tall but relaxed. Your feet should hit the ground beneath your hips, not your toes.
